Club Scoring Rules

1 Placement points are awarded based on how your team finishes. 1st place earns 100 pts, last place earns 70 pts — scaled linearly in between based on field size.
Formula: 100 − ((position − 1) ÷ (teams − 1)) × 30
2 Attendance: 20 pts just for showing up and playing the round.
3 Bonus points: 10 pts each for CTP (Closest to Pin) and LD (Longest Drive). Up to 4 bonus categories per round.
4 Season ranking = Average Points per round played. Lowest attendance isn’t penalised — attendance is already baked into each round’s score.
