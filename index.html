Pool Rules

1 Pick one golfer from each of the seven tiers (7 golfers total).
2 At the end of the tournament, your best four golfers' scores count towards your total.
3 The lowest total strokes wins the pool.
4 Any golfer who misses the cut receives a score of +80 strokes for each of Rounds 3 and 4.
Scoring Example

Rory McIlroy won last year's Masters with 277 total strokes. If he was one of your picks, 277 would be his contribution to your team total. Your best 4 of 7 golfers' scores are added together for your final score.
