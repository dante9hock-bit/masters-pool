Club Scoring Rules

1 Points are based on how many players play that round. First place receives points equal to the number of players. Second place receives (players - 2). Third place receives (players - 4). After third, each subsequent position drops by 1 point.
2 CTP (Closest to Pin) earns 1 bonus point per round.
3 LD (Longest Drive) earns 1 bonus point per round.
4 Attendance bonus: 0.5 points for every round played.
5 Final Score = Average Points per Round + Total Attendance Bonus. Highest score wins.
